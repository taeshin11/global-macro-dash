About Global Macro Dash

What is Global Macro Dash?

Global Macro Dash is a comprehensive, free dashboard displaying major exchange rates, commodity prices (gold, silver, oil), and key macroeconomic indicators. Designed for forex traders, financial analysts, economics students, international business professionals, and anyone tracking global markets.

Data Sources

  • Exchange Rates: Sourced from the European Central Bank (ECB) via the Frankfurter API, updated on business days.
  • Commodity Prices: Gold, silver, and crude oil data from market sources, updated periodically.
  • Macro Indicators: GDP growth, inflation, interest rates, and unemployment data compiled from IMF, World Bank, OECD, and national statistics offices.

Features

  • Live exchange rates for 9 major currency pairs
  • 7-day trend sparkline charts
  • Gold, silver, and crude oil price tracking
  • Interactive currency converter with 30+ currencies
  • Macro economic indicators for 12 major economies
  • Auto-refresh every 5 minutes
  • Available in 8 languages
