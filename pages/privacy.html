Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Global Macro Dash collects minimal data to improve the user experience:

  • Usage Data: Basic analytics such as page views, features used (converter, table sorting), language preference, and referral source.
  • Local Storage: Language preference and visitor count are stored locally in your browser.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information through the dashboard.

2. How We Use Information

Collected usage data is used solely to:

  • Understand which features are most used
  • Improve the dashboard experience
  • Monitor service health

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Frankfurter API: For exchange rate data (no user data shared)
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Adsterra: For displaying advertisements. See Adsterra's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: Hosting provider. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

Global Macro Dash itself does not set cookies. However, third-party advertising services (Google AdSense, Adsterra) may use cookies. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Retention

Local storage data remains on your device until you clear your browser data. Analytics data sent to our webhook is retained for up to 12 months.

6. Your Rights

You can clear all locally stored data at any time through your browser settings (Clear browsing data > Local Storage / Session Storage).
