Frequently Asked Questions

How often are exchange rates updated?

Exchange rates are fetched from the European Central Bank (ECB) via the Frankfurter API. Rates are updated on business days (Monday-Friday). The dashboard auto-refreshes every 5 minutes, but the underlying ECB data typically updates once per business day around 16:00 CET.

How accurate are the exchange rates?

Rates come from the ECB reference rates, which are widely used as a benchmark. However, actual trading rates may differ slightly depending on the market, broker, or financial institution. These rates are best used for informational purposes.

How do I use the currency converter?

Enter the amount you want to convert, select the source currency (From) and target currency (To), and the result will be calculated automatically. You can swap the currencies using the swap button. Quick conversion shortcuts are available below the converter.

What do the sparkline charts show?

The small charts on each currency card show the 7-day trend. Green indicates the rate has increased over the period, red indicates a decrease. Hover over the chart to see specific values.

Where does the macro indicator data come from?

Macro indicators (GDP, inflation, interest rates, unemployment) are compiled from public sources including the IMF, World Bank, OECD, and national statistics offices. This data is updated periodically and may not reflect the most recent changes.

Is Global Macro Dash free to use?

Yes, completely free. There are no premium features, subscriptions, or hidden costs. The service is supported by advertisements.

Can I use this for trading decisions?

Global Macro Dash is for informational and educational purposes only. It is not financial advice. Always consult qualified financial professionals and verify data with official sources before making trading or investment decisions.

What languages are supported?

The dashboard is available in 8 languages: English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified), Spanish, German, French, and Portuguese. Use the language selector in the header to switch languages.
