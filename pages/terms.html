2. Description of Service

Global Macro Dash is a free, web-based macro-economic data visualization dashboard. The Service displays the following information: live foreign exchange rates for major currency pairs sourced from the European Central Bank (ECB) via the Frankfurter API; indicative commodity prices for gold, silver, and crude oil; an interactive currency converter supporting over 30 currencies; 7-day trend sparkline charts for each currency pair; and a sortable macro-economic indicators table covering GDP growth, consumer price inflation, central bank interest rates, and unemployment rates for twelve major world economies. The Service is provided free of charge and is supported by third-party advertising. No account creation or payment is required to access any feature. The Service is offered "as is" and "as available" and we reserve the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue any feature at any time.
